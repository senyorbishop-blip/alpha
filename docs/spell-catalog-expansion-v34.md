# v34 spell catalog expansion

This pass expands the structured spell metadata catalog to deepen class support after the class/subclass sweeps.

## Added spell depth areas

### Arcane utility and identity
- Identify
- Find Familiar
- Chromatic Orb
- Telekinesis
- Leomund's Tiny Hut
- Seeming

### Divine / restoration / investigation
- Resistance
- Speak with Dead
- Commune
- Greater Restoration

### Primal control and battlefield identity
- Thorn Whip
- Faerie Fire
- Entangle
- Moonbeam
- Call Lightning
- Plant Growth
- Reincarnate

### Social / stealth / support coverage
- Friends
- Enhance Ability
- Scrying

## Goals of this pass
- Improve caster feel for Wizard, Sorcerer, Cleric, Druid, and Bard.
- Deepen Ranger and Warlock access lists where current catalog depth was thinner.
- Add more ritual, exploration, social, and long-form utility coverage instead of only attack/damage staples.
- Provide stronger subclass-supporting spells for stealth, divination, restoration, primal battlefield control, and party utility.

## Expected impact
- Better spell list identity in selection/managing views.
- More representative spell availability for class progression and subclass fantasy.
- Better support for future passes that attach richer spell cards, level-up picks, and subclass bonus spell handling.
